Pennsylvania Furries

Howdy!

It's been a fun 13 years running the Pennsylvania Furries website--I brought it online in 2005 and ran the website until the end of 2018.

The reason for taking the website down is largely because forums have been surpassed by other forms of communiaction such as Telegram, Twitter and Facebook, at least in the furry community. This is apparent by looking at user posts and user comments over the life of the website. It also didn't help that more and more spammers were signing up.

What comes next?

There are still plenty of ways to stay in touch with other Pennsylvania furries and participate in the community:

  • Discord

  • Facebook
    • Pennsylvania Furries Facebook Group

  • Other States
    • The Furst State - The Furst State or TFS is Delaware's regional furry community, though it has members from surrounding states as well such as Pennsylvania and Maryland.
    • Ohio Furry Events - An easy to access chat for all Ohio furries to post and find events hosted all throughout the state!
