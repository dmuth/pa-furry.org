Howdy!

It's been a fun 13 years running the Pennsylvania Furries website--I brought it online in 2005 and ran the website until the end of the 2018.

The reason for taking the website down is largely because forums have been surpassed by other forms of communiaction such as Telegram, Twitter and Facebook, at least in the furry community. This is apparent by looking at user posts and user comments over the life of the website. It also didn't helpthat more and more spammers were signing up.

What comes next?

There are still plenty of ways to stay in touch with other Pennsylvania furries and participate in the community:
  • Archived copy of this website
  • Pennsylvania Furries on Telegram
  • Lehigh Valley Furries on Telegram
  • Pennsylvania Furries Facebook Group
  • Pittsburgh Furries Facebook Group
  • Pennsylvania Furries Facebook Page
  • Western PA Furry Weekend (WPAFW) Facebook Page
  • Western PA Furry Weekend
  • The Furst State - The Furst State or TFS is Delaware's regional furry community, though it has members from surrounding states as well such as Pennsylvania and Maryland.
Oh, that sweet logo at the top of this page that adorned the website for all these years? That was graciously made by Omnibahumut.

If there are any questions or comments, feel free to reach out to me. I can be found on Twitter and Facebook.
